Case 01

GlobalLink Logistics

15× cost / order

ProblemWarehouse fulfilment speed dropped 12% in a quarter. Overtime rose. Output didn't.

ApproachGenerated a realistic ops dataset, normalised cost-per-order by shift, ran a Pareto cut on station cost.

ResultFound the real driver — Station C, not the night shift — and proposed an 80/20 restructuring plan at zero capital cost.
